Maritime Network Management System

A comprehensive solution for monitoring and managing maritime vessel systems in compliance with the Korean Register's "Guidance for Cyber Resilience of Ships and Systems" (GC-44-K, 2024)

Cyber Resilience for Modern Vessels

Maritime vessels increasingly rely on interconnected computer-based systems for navigation, propulsion, power management, and other critical functions. This Network Management System (NMS) provides a comprehensive platform to monitor and secure these systems according to international standards.

Our system is built to meet the requirements of IACS UR E26 (Cyber Resilience of Ships) and E27 (Cyber Resilience of Onboard Systems and Equipment) as implemented in the Korean Register's guidance.

Key Features

Vessel Asset Inventory

Security Zone Management

Advanced Alerting

Real-time alerts for security incidents, threshold violations, and system anomalies.

Role-Based Access Control

Real-Time Monitoring

Live visualization of system performance, network traffic, and security status.

SDLC Documentation

Comprehensive documentation for Secure Development Life Cycle compliance.

Regulatory Compliance

This NMS is designed to help vessel operators meet the following standards:

  • Korean Register's "Guidance for Cyber Resilience of Ships and Systems" (GC-44-K, 2024)
  • IACS UR E26 - Cyber Resilience of Ships
  • IACS UR E27 - Cyber Resilience of Onboard Systems and Equipment
  • IMO MSC-FAL.1/Circ.3 - Guidelines on Maritime Cyber Risk Management
